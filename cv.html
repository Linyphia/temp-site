Current Research

Ongoing field and lab studies of the Sierra dome spider sexual selection system at Flathead Lake Biological Station. Current foci:

  • Manipulative, honest-vague signaling by females
  • Copulatory courtship energetics (CO‚ÇÇ respirometry)
  • Sexually antagonistic genes and disease/lifespan/behavioral evolution
  • eDNA-PCR and metagenomics: how microbiomes shape mate choice
Main emphasis: male personality development matched to female preference strategies.

Human lab research tests the Parasite Stress Hypothesis vs. my Informational Boundaries Hypothesis for religiosity using data from 537 subjects. Developing a novel evolutionary explanation for the placebo effect based on honest signaling and commitment theory ‚Äî with implications for compassionate care.
